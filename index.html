Currency Converter Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Currency Converter is a simple, client-side tool for converting currencies. We respect your privacy and designed the app to avoid collecting personal data or persisting data on your device.

We do not sell data. We do not track you.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage analytics, or device identifiers.

3. How the App Works

  • Conversion calculations run locally on your device; results are not stored in persistent storage.
  • If the app uses exchange rates from a public API, your browser fetches rates directly; we do not receive or log those requests.
  • No cookies, trackers, or ads are embedded.

4. Third-Party Services

If future versions integrate an exchange-rate API (e.g., European Central Bank or similar), that provider’s privacy policy will apply to requests sent from your browser. We will select providers that do not require personal data.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect data, we do not maintain user data stores. Standard web hosting security practices are followed for the static site.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, but it is safe to use because no personal data is collected.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect minor product or legal changes. The latest version will always be published on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.

8. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at suzuki.coding.19@gmail.com.

Currency Converter は通貨換算を行うシンプルなクライアントサイドのツールです。お客様のプライバシーを尊重し、個人データを収集したり端末に情報を保存しない設計にしています。

2. 収集するデータ

個人情報、利用分析データ、デバイス識別子は収集・保存・送信しません。

3. アプリの動作

将来、為替レート API（例：欧州中央銀行など）を統合する場合、リクエストにはその提供者のプライバシーポリシーが適用されます。個人データを必要としない提供者を選定します。

当方はデータを収集しないため、ユーザーデータの保管は行っていません。静的サイトの一般的なセキュリティ慣行を遵守しています。
